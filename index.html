Getit TOGETHER.

A simplified event planning app, aimed to help you quickly plan
an event with out taking time out of your busy life.

FEATURES

CALENDAR

GiT makes it easy to create, select,
and switch a date for any event, as
well as invite your friends. You all
just enter in your availability and
we'll sort out the best time for
you to meet.

LOCATION

Share your location with your friends
to find out whose nearby and get
recommendations on where to go.

CHAT

Chat about it; talk to the people
within your event about time,
weather, food, drinks, and so on.

Watch and see how easy it is!
